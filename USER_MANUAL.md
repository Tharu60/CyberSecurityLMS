## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Welcome
Welcome to the Cybersecurity Learning Management System! This platform provides a structured learning path through 6 stages of cybersecurity education, from basics to advanced topics.

### 1.2 System Requirements

**Browser Requirements:**
- Chrome 90+
- Firefox 88+
- Safari 14+
- Edge 90+

**Device Compatibility:**
- Desktop computers (Windows, macOS, Linux)
- Tablets (iPad, Android tablets)
- Mobile phones (iOS, Android)

**Internet Connection:**
- Stable internet required for video streaming
- Minimum 5 Mbps recommended

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Student** | Take assessments, watch videos, track progress, earn certificates |
| **Instructor** | Create and manage content (videos, questions) |

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Creating an Account

**Step 1:** Navigate to the registration page

**Step 2:** Fill in the registration form:
- **Full Name:** Your complete name
- **Email:** Valid email address (used for login)

**Step 2:** Enter your credentials:

**Step 4:** You'll be redirected to your role-specific dashboard

**Forgot Your Password?**

### 2.3 Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your dashboard with:

**Stage Cards (Main Section):**

### 3.2 Taking the Initial Assessment

**Purpose:** The initial assessment determines your starting stage based on your current knowledge level.

**How It Works:**

**Step 1:** Click "Start Initial Assessment" button

**Step 2:** Answer 25 multiple-choice questions
- Read each question carefully
- Select one option (A, B, C, or D)
- Use "Next" and "Previous" buttons to navigate

**Step 3:** Progress tracking
- Progress dots show your position (Question X/25)
- All questions must be answered before submission

**Step 4:** Submit assessment
- "Submit" button appears on the last question
- Click "Submit" when ready

**Step 5:** View results
- Your score (e.g., 18/25)
- Percentage (e.g., 72%)
- Starting stage assignment
- Detailed answer breakdown

**Stage Placement:**
- **Less than 25%:** Start at Stage 1 (Basics)
- **25-50%:** Start at Stage 2 (Intermediate)
- **50-75%:** Start at Stage 3 (Advanced)
- **75% or higher:** Start at Stage 4 (Expert)

**Tips:**
- Take your time, no time limit
- Answer honestly to get accurate placement
- You can navigate back to review questions
- This assessment can only be taken once

### 3.3 Learning in Stages

Each stage contains:
1. Educational videos
2. A quiz assessment

**Stage Progression:**
1. Complete videos (optional but recommended)
2. Take the quiz
3. Pass with 60% or higher
4. Next stage unlocks automatically

### 3.4 Watching Videos

**Step 1:** Click on an unlocked stage card

**Step 2:** You'll see the Stage View page with:
- **Left Side:** Video playlist (2 videos per stage)
- **Right Side:** YouTube video player

**Step 3:** Click on a video to play it
- Video loads in the embedded player
- Watch at your own pace
- No viewing requirements

**Step 4:** Mark video as complete
- Click "Mark as Complete" button after watching
- Checkmark appears next to completed videos
- Completion percentage updates

**Tips:**
- Videos provide important context for quizzes
- You can rewatch videos anytime
- Take notes while watching
- All videos are free and accessible

### 3.5 Taking Stage Quizzes

**Quiz Details:**
- **Stages 1-4:** 15 questions each
- **Stage 5 (Final):** 25 questions
- **Passing Score:** 60% (9/15 or 15/25)
- **Retakes:** Unlimited attempts allowed

**How to Take a Quiz:**

**Step 1:** From Stage View, click "Take Quiz" button

**Step 2:** Quiz interface loads
- One question at a time
- Four options (A, B, C, D)
- Question counter (e.g., "Question 3/15")

**Step 3:** Answer questions
- Select one answer per question
- Click "Next" to proceed
- Click "Previous" to review

**Step 4:** Submit quiz
- "Submit" button appears on last question
- Confirm your submission

**Step 5:** View results

**If you passed (60% or higher):**
- Success message: "YOU PASSED!"
- Your score and percentage
- Detailed answer breakdown
- Next stage unlocks automatically
- "Continue to Dashboard" button

**If you failed (below 60%):**
- Message: "YOU FAILED"
- Your score and percentage
- Required score (60%)
- "Retry Quiz" button
- Encouragement message

**Tips:**
- Read questions carefully
- Eliminate obviously wrong answers
- Review all answers before submitting
- Learn from mistakes when retrying
- Videos contain helpful information

### 3.6 Retrying Failed Quizzes

**Step 1:** From results modal, click "Retry Quiz"

**Step 2:** Quiz restarts with same questions
- Previous answers are NOT saved
- Fresh attempt
- Attempt number increments

**Step 3:** Complete quiz again

**No Limit:** You can retry as many times as needed to pass

**Progress Tracking:** All attempts are recorded in your history

### 3.8 Generating Your Certificate

**Eligibility Requirements:**
- Complete all 5 main stages (Stage 1 through Stage 5)
- Pass each stage with 60% or higher
- Only one certificate per student

**How to Generate:**

**Step 1:** Navigate to Certificate page (after completing all stages)

**Step 2:** Check eligibility status
- Green button: "Generate Certificate" (eligible)
- Disabled button with message (not eligible)

**Step 3:** Click "Generate Certificate"

**Step 4:** Certificate generates with:
- Your name
- Course name: "Cybersecurity LMS Course"
- Issue date
- Unique verification code (16 characters)

**Step 5:** View and use certificate
- Professional landscape design
- Print to paper or save as PDF
- Share verification code with employers

**Verification:**
- Public verification URL provided
- Anyone can verify authenticity using your code
- No login required for verification

**Print Certificate:**
1. Click "Print Certificate" button
2. Browser print dialog opens
3. Choose printer or "Save as PDF"
4. Adjust print settings if needed
5. Print/Save

## 6. Troubleshooting

### 6.1 Login Issues

**Problem:** Cannot login

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Credentials**
   - Verify email is correct
   - Check password (case-sensitive)
   - Ensure caps lock is off

2. **Reset Password**
   - Feature coming soon
   - Contact administrator for manual reset

3. **Account Not Found**
   - Verify you registered
   - Check email for typos
   - Register if needed

4. **Token Expired**
   - If logged in for 7+ days, login again
   - Token expires after 7 days

### 6.2 Registration Issues

**Problem:** Cannot register

**Solutions:**

1. **Email Already Registered**
   - Use different email
   - Or login with existing account

2. **Government ID Error**
   - Format must be exactly XXXX/12345
   - 4 uppercase letters, slash, 5 digits
   - Example: TEST/12345
   - Must be unique

3. **Password Too Short**
   - Minimum 6 characters required
   - Use stronger password

4. **Passwords Don't Match**
   - Retype both fields
   - Ensure they match exactly

### 6.3 Quiz Issues

**Problem:** Cannot submit quiz

**Solutions:**
1. **Not All Questions Answered**
   - Check progress dots
   - Answer all questions
   - Submit button enabled only when complete

2. **Network Error**
   - Check internet connection
   - Retry submission
   - Answers saved in browser

3. **Quiz Not Loading**
   - Refresh page
   - Clear browser cache
   - Try different browser

**Problem:** Stage not unlocking after passing

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh dashboard
2. Logout and login again
3. Check that score was 60% or higher
4. Contact administrator if issue persists

### 6.4 Video Issues

**Problem:** Video won't play

**Solutions:**
1. **YouTube Blocked**
   - Check if YouTube is accessible
   - Try on different network
   - Use VPN if necessary

2. **Video Removed**
   - Contact instructor
   - Video may be unavailable
   - Instructor can update link

3. **Slow Loading**
   - Check internet speed
   - Lower video quality in YouTube player
   - Wait for buffering

**Problem:** Cannot mark video complete

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh page and try again
3. Button should appear below video player

### 6.5 Certificate Issues

**Problem:** Cannot generate certificate

**Solutions:**
1. **Not Eligible**
   - Complete all 5 main stages (1-5)
   - Must pass each with 60%
   - Check dashboard for incomplete stages

2. **Certificate Already Generated**
   - Can only generate once
   - View existing certificate on certificate page

3. **Generation Failed**
   - Check internet connection
   - Retry generation
   - Contact administrator if persists

**Problem:** Cannot print certificate

**Solutions:**
1. Use "Print Certificate" button
2. In print dialog, select "Save as PDF"
3. Or print directly to printer
4. Try different browser if issues persist

### 6.6 General Issues

**Problem:** Page not loading

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh browser (F5 or Cmd+R)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try incognito/private mode
4. Try different browser
5. Check internet connection

**Problem:** Slow performance

**Solutions:**
1. Close unnecessary tabs
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check internet speed
4. Restart browser
5. Update browser to latest version

**Problem:** Mobile display issues

**Solutions:**
1. Use mobile browser (Safari, Chrome)
2. Rotate device (portrait/landscape)
3. Zoom in if text too small
4. Update browser

## 7. FAQs

### 7.1 General Questions

**Q: Is the LMS free to use?**
A: Contact your organization for pricing/access information.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+

**Q: Can I use on mobile?**
A: Yes! The platform is fully responsive and works on phones and tablets.

**Q: How long does it take to complete?**
A: Depends on your pace. Average students complete in 2-4 weeks.

**Q: Is there a time limit?**
A: No time limits on courses or quizzes. Learn at your own pace.

### 7.2 Student Questions

**Q: Can I skip stages?**
A: Initial assessment determines starting stage. High scorers can skip early stages.

**Q: How many times can I retry a quiz?**
A: Unlimited retries. Keep trying until you pass!

**Q: What happens if I fail a quiz?**
A: You can immediately retry. Review videos and study before retrying.

**Q: Are videos mandatory?**
A: No, but highly recommended. They contain information helpful for quizzes.

**Q: Can I retake the initial assessment?**
A: No, initial assessment can only be taken once for placement.

**Q: What's the passing score?**
A: 60% for all quizzes (9/15 or 15/25 depending on stage).

**Q: How do I get a certificate?**
A: Complete all 5 main stages (1-5) with passing scores, then generate on certificate page.

**Q: Is the certificate official?**
A: Verification code allows employers to verify authenticity.

**Q: Can I change my starting stage?**
A: No, determined by initial assessment. But you progress through all stages.

### 7.3 Instructor Questions

**Q: How many videos can I add?**
A: No limit. Add as many as needed for comprehensive learning.

**Q: Can I edit questions after students take quiz?**
A: Yes, but existing scores remain. Only future quizzes use updated version.

**Q: What video platforms are supported?**
A: Currently only YouTube videos.

**Q: Can I see individual student answers?**
A: Yes, in analytics tab you can view detailed student performance.

**Q: How do I organize video order?**
A: Use order_number field (1, 2, 3...). Auto-assigned if left blank.

### 7.4 Administrator Questions

**Q: How do I create admin accounts?**
A: Currently admin accounts created manually in database. Registration only allows student/instructor.

**Q: Can I restore deleted users?**
A: No, deletion is permanent. Be cautious.

**Q: How do I backup data?**
A: See Maintenance Manual for backup procedures.

**Q: Can I export statistics?**
A: Feature coming soon. Currently view on dashboard.

**Q: What if a student loses certificate?**
A: They can view it anytime on certificate page. Code is permanent.

### 7.5 Technical Questions

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: Securely on the LMS server. Passwords are encrypted.

**Q: How long are JWT tokens valid?**
A: 7 days. You'll need to login again after expiration.

**Q: Is my password encrypted?**
A: Yes, using bcrypt hashing. Never stored as plain text.

**Q: Can I use offline?**
A: No, internet connection required for all features.

**Q: What data is collected?**
A: Account info, quiz scores, progress data, video completion. No sensitive personal data beyond what you provide.
